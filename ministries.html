Are you interested in serving but don't know where to start?

Our church is blessed to be able to serve God and our community in many different ways. We firmly believe in bearing one another's burdens, encouraging and uplifting one another, and living out our faith and our love for our Saviour. We seek to serve in order that our lives might show His love.

We believe the future lies in the hands of our children, and so do all we can to lead them in the right paths, teach them the right way, and prepare them to face the world as soldiers and servants for Christ. From the nursery to the young adults' group, we seek to provide a safe environment where they can learn about God, the true way to salvation, and what it means to live as Christians.

We are grateful to be able to serve and reach out to our community through our pantry ministry as well as our Saturday Afternoon Visitation.

We know prayer to be our greatest resource and so commit to pray for one another, for our missionaries, for the persecuted church, and for our community on a regular basis.

If you would like to join any of these ministries in any capacity, talk to our pastor. Serving God together is one of the greatest blessings you will find.
