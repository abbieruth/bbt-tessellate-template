Organizations

Bibles for Muslims

Bibles for Muslims is an organization dedicated to reaching Muslims in Canada. For more information, see their website here.

Bibles for Russia

Bibles for Russia is a Christian foreign missions board, forming short-terms missions teams to plant and grow churches in the former Soviet Union. For more information, view their website here.

Shenandoah Boys' Ranch

The Shenandoad Ranch for Boys, a ministry of Shenandoah Baptist Church, is a place where struggling young men, ages 13 to 18, can find intervention, help, encouragement, and spiritual guidance. Here they will learn to find the answers to their questions in God's Word. Learn more here.

Faith International Partners

Faith International Partners is an organization dedicated to bringing the Gospel to the 10/40 window where God's Word is severely restricted and Christians are harshly persecuted.
